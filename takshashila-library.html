About us

Built for focus, not features

Takshashila takes its name from the world's earliest known university — a place students travelled far to reach, for the sake of serious study. Our library in Katra, Prayagraj keeps that same idea simple: a calm, disciplined room with long hours and no distractions.

Whether you're preparing for UPSC, PCS, SSC, or your board exams, the space is built around one job — helping you sit down and actually study.
